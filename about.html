Joe's Philosophy

"AI doesn't have to be complicated. It doesn't have to be expensive. It doesn't even have to work perfectly. It just has to work better than Steve from Marketing trying to use Excel. And frankly, that's a low bar."
‚Äî Joe, probably 2023

Joe's approach to AI consulting is simple: strip away the buzzwords, ignore the hype cycles, and focus on solutions that actually solve problems. Sometimes those solutions involve machine learning. Sometimes they involve a well-placed Google Sheet. And sometimes, they involve telling the client that their problem isn't actually a problem.

This philosophy has led to a 60% client retention rate (the other 40% realized they didn't actually need AI and thanked Joe for his honesty before leaving).
